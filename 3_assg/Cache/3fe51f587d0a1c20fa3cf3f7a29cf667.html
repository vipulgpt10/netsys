International Symposium

The Symposium Program is now available

 

Funded by Mesoaqua project

 

Confirmed Invited Speakers

Scope

The aim of the symposium is to exchange ideas and experiences from experimental work using mesocosms as the basic tool and present the state of the art in mesocosm-based research as well as a definition of remaining gaps and priorities for future actions.
